Why Marine Life Matters

Marine ecosystems support biodiversity, provide food, and help regulate our climate. Threats like pollution, overfishing, and climate change put our oceans at risk. Kaitiakitanga reminds us of our responsibility to protect these taonga (treasures) for all.
